About Me

I’m a Johannesburg‑based economist with 10+ years’ experience analysing health policy and financing across Africa. My work blends rigorous quantitative & qualitative research with hands‑on stakeholder engagement to advance Universal Health Coverage, gender‑responsive financing, and HIV prevention. I’m currently exploring Generative AI to streamline research workflows—turning hours of data wrangling, report drafting, and literature reviews into minutes.

Professional Snapshot

  • Current role: Post‑Doctoral Research Fellow, University of Cape Town (2021 – 2024)
  • Expertise: Economic evaluation • Impact evaluation • Quantitative & qualitative analysis • Project management (CAPM)
  • Past advisory: Health Financing Technical Advisor to Ministries of Health in Malawi & Sierra Leone (ODI Fellowship)
  • Publications: 20+ peer‑reviewed papers in health economics & policy

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity
Live Online Certification · 8 weeks · No‑Code AI Tools for Task Automation (May 2025)

Still copy‑pasting into ChatGPT? This hands‑on programme shows how to embed AI directly into everyday workflows. Over eight weeks I’ll build automations and prototypes that free up time for higher‑value thinking.

  • Automate document, spreadsheet & data tasks that drain hours
  • Organise knowledge bases and streamline literature reviews with AI
  • Develop custom chatbots for project or team support
  • Create professional & scientific visuals and presentations, fast
  • Apply new skills in a capstone project that tackles a real workplace need
  • Get live guidance in workshops, help sessions & online forums
