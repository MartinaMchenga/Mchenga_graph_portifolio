My work blends rigorous quantitative & qualitative research with hands‑on stakeholder engagement to advance Universal Health Coverage, gender‑responsive financing, and HIV prevention. I’m currently exploring Generative AI to streamline research workflows—turning hours of data wrangling, report drafting, and literature reviews into minutes.
